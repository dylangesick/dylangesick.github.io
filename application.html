Masked Special Forces Web Game

Masked Special Forces is an intense first-person shooter available directly in your browser. Step into the boots of a special ops soldier and engage in thrilling multiplayer battles or solo missions against AI enemies.

Game Overview

The game offers a variety of weapons, maps, and customization options. Players can select different characters and gear, participate in various game modes, and test their skills against global opponents in real time.

How to Play

  1. Use the WASD keys to move around the battlefield.
  2. Use the mouse to aim and left-click to fire your weapon.
  3. Press R to reload when you're low on ammo.
  4. Choose a game mode (Multiplayer or Solo) from the main menu.
  5. Complete missions and eliminate enemies to earn points.

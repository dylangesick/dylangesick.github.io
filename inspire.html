What makes a hero?

A hero is someone who demonstrates courage, selflessness, and a willingness to fight for what is right, often at personal cost. In the case of Spider-Man (Peter Parker), he embodies these traits in several ways. He uses his powers to protect his city and its citizens, despite the personal sacrifices it often demands.

"With great power comes great responsibility." This sense of responsibility and his determination to make the world a better place, even when it's difficult or dangerous for him, defines Spider-Man as a true hero.
